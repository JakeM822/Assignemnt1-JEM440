Batman Character Bio

  • Name: Bruce Wayne
  • Height: 6' 2"
  • Weight: 210-220 LBS
  • First Appearance: Detective Comics #27
  • Special Abilities: Master Martial Artist, Superior Intellect, Master Detective, Stealth Capabilities, Technical Expertise
  • Origin: Batman, born Bruce Wayne, dedicated his life to fighting crime after witnessing the tragic murder of his parents in Gotham City. Without superpowers, he relies on peak physical training, detective skills, and advanced technology to battle injustice. Using the persona of Batman, he strikes fear into criminals while upholding a strict moral code to protect his city.
